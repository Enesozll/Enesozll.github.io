Gayem, siber güvenlikte etkili ve güvenilir bir uzman olmak. I am Muhammed Enes, born in Şanlıurfa in May 2001 and I am a 4th year student at Fırat University, Department of Digital Forensics Engineering. I have a strong passion in the field of cyber security, especially offensive security, and I increase my competencies by participating in various camps and certification programs to improve myself in this field. My career goal is to specialize in web application security and penetration testing. In this direction, I progress by continuously receiving training and gaining experience. My goal is to be an effective and reliable expert in cyber security.
